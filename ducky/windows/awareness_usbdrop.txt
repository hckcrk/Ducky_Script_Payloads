DELAY 700

DELAY 1200

STRINGLN If this had been a real attack it could have run commands as you.
STRINGLN
STRINGLN - Do not plug in USB drives you find or do not trust.
STRINGLN - Lock your screen when you step away (WIN+L).
STRINGLN - Report dropped/unknown USB devices to your security team.
