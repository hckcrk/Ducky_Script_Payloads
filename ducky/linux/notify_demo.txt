REM Title:  HID + notify-send demo (Linux)
REM Class:  HID injection / living-off-the-land. Benign.
REM Effect: shows a desktop notification. Nothing is saved or sent.
REM Use:    authorized training / awareness demos only.
DEFAULT_DELAY 40
DELAY 1000
CTRL ALT t
DELAY 1500
STRING notify-send "Security Awareness Test" "A USB device ran this. Do not trust unknown USB devices." 2>/dev/null; echo "notification sent"
ENTER
