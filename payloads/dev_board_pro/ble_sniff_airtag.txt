# ble_sniff_airtag -- scan for Apple AirTag / Find My BLE advertisements
# Target: AWOK Dual Touch V3, orange (CLI) port, esp32_marauder_dev_board_pro.bin, v1.13.0
# Passive BLE scan (ESP32-WROOM has Bluetooth). Prints detected AirTags.
# Duration: ~45 s.  SD: optional (for logging).  GPS: not required.

stopscan
sniffbt -t airtag
DELAY 45000
stopscan
