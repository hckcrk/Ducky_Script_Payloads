# beacon_sniff -- passively capture 802.11 beacon frames to an SD pcap
# Target: Marauder v6 / v6.1 (ESP32-WROOM + microSD), esp32_marauder_v6_1.bin, v1.13.0
# Passive collection only (no transmit). Writes a .pcap on the SD card.
# Duration: ~45 s.  SD: required to save the capture.  GPS: not required.

stopscan
sniffbeacon
DELAY 45000
stopscan
